We recommend the Indica strain
Sit back, relax, and inhale the mellow body high of the Indica family. Great for people with physical pain or trouble sleeping, Indicas are bred to transform your body into a calm, peaceful state.
We recommend the Sativa strain
Great for those with depression or anxiety, the Sativa strain usually provides a bubbly, giddy good time. Similar to an alcohol buzz, the Sativa experience is a happy high focused above the neck.
We recommend the hybrid strain
Popular among heavy stoners and newbies alike, hybrid strains allow you to enjoy the best of both Indica and Sativa highs without the extremes. Plus, the wide variety of hybrid varieties makes it is easy to "customize" your cannabis experience.
